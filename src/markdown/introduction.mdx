Mina Bridge is a bridge service that provides access to inter-blockchain liquidity for Mina and brings assets from other chains into the Mina ecosystem. The platform allows users to transfer crypto-assets in and out of Mina chain to other blockchains.

The initial version will support Ethereum to Mina which will allow

- Ethereum to Mina and receive Wrapped Ethereum (WETH)
- Mina (WETH) to Ethereum
